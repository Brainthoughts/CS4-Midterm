Start and update the web app by running ./start.sh from inside the group6/CS4-Midterm folder.
Alternatively, run node app.js to start without updating.
Navigate to http://www.mvhscsf.org:4006/ and begin your journey through the 10 puzzles, using out-of-the-box thinking to solve them.
Progress is saved as long as you have at least one browser tab open with the site.
The URLs and flags are randomized each time therefore you cannot share links to levels with other players.
Your playthrough will be timed and your final time will be displayed to you upon completion of all levels.
Try racing your friends to see who can get the fastest time.
